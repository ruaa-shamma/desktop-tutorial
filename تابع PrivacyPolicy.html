Privacy Policy

At Glow Hana, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase from our store.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address)
  • Payment details (processed securely via trusted third-party payment gateways)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Website usage data, such as pages visited and time spent on our site

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share it only with trusted third parties such as payment processors and shipping companies to complete your orders. All third parties are required to protect your data.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Cookies

Glow Hana uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us using the information below.
