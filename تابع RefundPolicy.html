Refund Policy

At Glow Hana, your satisfaction is our priority. We understand that sometimes things don’t work out, and we’re here to help. Please read our refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused, unopened, and in its original packaging.
  • The return request must be made within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required.

2. Non-Refundable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Opened or used skincare and makeup products
  • Items purchased on sale or with promotional discounts
  • Gift cards

3. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the product and packaging. We will either issue a full refund or send a replacement at no additional cost.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at support@glowhana.com with your order number and the reason for your return. Our team will review your request and respond within 2-3 business days.

5. Refund Process

Once your return is approved and the item is received and inspected, we will notify you. If approved, the refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 5–10 business days.

6. Return Shipping

You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (e.g., incorrect or damaged items).
